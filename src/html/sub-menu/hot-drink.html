Espresso

Espresso is a type of coffee that contains more caffeine by volume than most coffees, which is why it is served in small cups. It is not that it has more caffeine than the rest, but that it is concentrated in less liquid.

Traditional

Latte

The word Latte comes from Italian and means "coffee with milk", however the preparation is what distinguishes this drink, because its process can be considered an art, it is a preparation based on espresso coffee and steamed milk.

Mocha

It is a preparation that enters into the coffees with milk to which traditionally a part of cocoa or chocolate syrup is added.

Cappuccino

It's the perfect balance between espresso, steamed milk and foam.
